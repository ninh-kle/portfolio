Survey Form

About

This is a survey form. The topic of they survey was left open-ended. For this project I chose somewhat of a dark humor approach. When I previously created this survey form we were in the midst of a very heated presidential election in the middle of a global pandemic. I was trying to capture the emotions felt by millions of American citizens. As for the functionality of this page it incorperated the use of simple radio buttons, a selection menu, check list, even a text area for additional information, and a submit button.

Product Landing Page

About

This is a product landing page. The original project called for a landing page for a product of my choosing. I had a difficult time selecting a specific product to market. Instead I chose to do an online ordering system for my favorite food in the world: hot dogs. It's always been my dream that if I were able to retire do anything else I would open up my own hot dog stand. As for the functionality of this page it features a navigation bar, embedded video, data input fields, and responsive design using BootStrap. It also includes some of the previous things I learned such as: check lists, selection menues, and a submit button.
